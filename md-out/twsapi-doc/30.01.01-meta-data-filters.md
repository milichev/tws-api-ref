### Meta Data Filters

While this list contains an array of Meta Data filters that may be used, please be aware that new values may be made available or removed without notice.

In addition to the EClient.reqWshMetaData field being mandatory prior to the [EClient.reqWshEventData()](30.02-event-data.md) function, the JSON content will also return the appropriate column values that are returned along with the function.
