### YIELD\_LAST

**Bar Values:**

| Open | High | Low | Close | Volume |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Starting last yield | Highest last yield | Lowest last yield | Last last yield | N/A |

**Supported Products:** Indices

**Note:** Yield historical data only available for corporate bonds.
