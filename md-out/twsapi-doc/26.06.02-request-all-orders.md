### All submitted orders

#### 26.06.02 All submitted orders

Requests all current open orders in associated accounts at the current moment. The existing orders will be received via the [openOrder](#open-order) and [orderStatus](26.05-order-status.md) events. Open orders are returned once; this function does not initiate a subscription.

self.reqAllOpenOrders()
