### Request Contract Details

#### 16.03.01 Request Contract Details

**reqId:** int. Request identifier to track data.

**contract:** ContractDetails. the contract used as sample to query the available contracts.  
Typically contains at least the Symbol, SecType, Exchange, and Currency.  
)

Upon requesting EClient.reqContractDetails, all contracts matching the requested [Contract Object](../twsapi-ref/06-contract-ref.md) will be returned to [EWrapper.contractDetails](16.03.02-receive-contract-details.md) or [EWrapper.bondContractDetails](16.03.03-receive-bond-details.md).

self.reqContractDetails(reqId, contract)
